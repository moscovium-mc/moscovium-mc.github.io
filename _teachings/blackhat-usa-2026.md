## Event Overview

Black Hat USA 2026 continues the tradition of the world's most technical and relevant information security event, established in 1997. This six-day event provides:

- **Technical Briefings**: Peer-reviewed presentations from leading security researchers on zero-day vulnerabilities, exploit techniques, and defensive strategies
- **Hands-On Training**: In-depth courses taught by industry experts on offensive security, forensics, reverse engineering, and emerging threats
- **Black Hat Arsenal**: Live demonstrations of open-source security tools by their creators
- **Business Hall**: Exhibition featuring security vendors, startups, and recruitment opportunities
- **Networking**: Exclusive events connecting researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders

## Event Structure

### Training (August 2-3)
- Two-day immersive courses (separate registration and fee)
- Topics include: Advanced Penetration Testing, Cloud Security, Threat Hunting, Reverse Engineering, Incident Response
- Limited seating; early registration recommended

### Main Conference (August 4-6)
- **Technical Briefings**: Multiple tracks running simultaneously
- **Business Hall**: Vendor exhibitions and product demonstrations
- **Arsenal**: Open-source tool showcases throughout the conference
- **Special Events**: Evening receptions, community meetups, and awards

## Registration Tiers

- **Training + Briefings Pass**: Full access to all training and conference content
- **Briefings Only Pass**: Access to main conference days (Aug 4-6)
- **Business Hall Pass**: Limited access to exhibition floor (free registration)
- **Media/Analyst Pass**: Available for qualified press and industry analysts

*Note: Training sessions require separate registration and typically sell out months in advance.*
