## Conference Overview

ShmooCon is the premier East Coast hacker conference, known for its intimate atmosphere, technical depth, and strong sense of community. Unlike the massive Vegas conferences, ShmooCon maintains a cozy, family-like vibe while delivering world-class security content.

## Conference Highlights

- **Build It, Break It, Fix It**: Programming contest with real security challenges
- **Crypto and Privacy Village**: Deep dives into cryptography and privacy tech
- **Lockpick Village**: Hands-on physical security training
- **Ghost in the Shellcode**: CTF competition (when available)
- **Lightning Talks**: Rapid-fire presentations on diverse topics

## Why Attend

- **Smaller crowds** mean better access to speakers and networking
- **DC location** attracts government, military, and policy folks
- **Winter timing** makes it a great "kickoff" to the conference year
- **Strong emphasis on education** and skill-building
- **Family-friendly policies** and inclusive environment

## Tracks & Content

- Offensive Security & Exploitation
- Defensive Security & Blue Team
- Cryptography & Privacy
- Hardware Hacking & Embedded Systems
- Web Application Security
- Network Security & Protocol Analysis
- Social Engineering & Physical Security
- Career Development & Industry Trends

## Pro Tips

- **Badges typically go on sale in October** and sell out quickly
- **Washington Hilton** is centrally located with easy Metro access
- **DC winter weather** can be cold—pack accordingly
- **Hotel blocks fill up fast**—book early or stay nearby
- **Badge Price**: ~$250 (varies by purchase timing)

ShmooCon has a reputation for being welcoming to newcomers while still satisfying veteran hackers. The conference maintains the spirit of early hacker conventions while delivering cutting-edge content.
