## Event Overview

RSA Conference 2026 continues the tradition of the world's most influential cybersecurity event, established in 1991. This four-day conference provides:

- **Keynote Presentations**: Industry visionaries and thought leaders sharing insights on emerging threats and security trends
- **Breakout Sessions**: Hundreds of sessions across multiple tracks including Security Strategy, Threat Detection & Response, Cloud Security, and Privacy
- **Expo Hall**: 500+ exhibitors showcasing cutting-edge security products, services, and solutions
- **Innovation Sandbox**: Competition featuring 10 emerging cybersecurity startups pitching to industry judges
- **Networking Opportunities**: Exclusive events connecting security professionals, vendors, and industry leaders
- **Hands-On Labs**: Interactive workshops providing practical experience with security tools and techniques

## Innovation Sandbox Contest

The annual Innovation Sandbox contest showcases 10 of the most promising cybersecurity startups. Each company presents a 3-minute pitch to a panel of industry judges. The winner is recognized as "The Most Innovative Startup" at RSA Conference.

- **Application Period**: Opens August 2025
- **Finalists Announced**: January 2026
- **Contest Date**: Tuesday, March 24, 2026
- **Past Winners**: Include companies like Imperva, Sourcefire, and Cylance
