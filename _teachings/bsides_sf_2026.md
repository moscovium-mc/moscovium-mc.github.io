## Conference Overview

Security BSides San Francisco brings the BSides community spirit to the heart of the Bay Area tech scene. This free, one-day conference offers technical content, networking, and community building for security professionals and enthusiasts.

## Conference Highlights

- **Technical presentations** from local and visiting researchers
- **Lightning talks** on emerging topics and quick tips
- **Networking** with Bay Area security community
- **No cost to attend**—just register and show up
- **Intimate venue** encourages interaction and discussion

## Bay Area Advantage

Being in San Francisco means access to speakers and attendees from major tech companies, startups, and research institutions. The content often reflects the innovative spirit of the region, with talks on:
- Cloud security and DevSecOps
- AI/ML security and adversarial machine learning
- API security and microservices protection
- Startup security challenges and solutions
- Cutting-edge research from local universities

## Community Focus

BSides SF emphasizes:
- **Inclusivity** and welcoming newcomers
- **Knowledge sharing** without commercial pressure
- **Collaboration** between industry, academia, and independent researchers
- **Mentorship** opportunities for students and early-career professionals
- **Diversity** in speakers and attendees

## Pro Tips

- **Free but registration required** due to venue capacity limits
- **Typically announces details in January**—watch their website and social media
- **Great opportunity to connect** with the Bay Area security scene
- **Often held near major tech companies**—easy access via public transit
- **Badge Price**: FREE

BSides SF is perfect for anyone in the Bay Area looking to connect with the local security community, learn new skills, or get involved in the broader security ecosystem.
