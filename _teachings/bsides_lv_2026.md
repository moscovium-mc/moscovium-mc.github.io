## Conference Overview

Security BSides Las Vegas is the original and largest BSides event, running alongside DEF CON week in Las Vegas. As a free, community-driven conference, BSides LV provides an accessible entry point to the Vegas security scene while delivering high-quality content.

## What to Expect

- **Single-day format** (perfect for DEF CON week scheduling)
- **Mix of technical talks** and community-focused presentations
- **Networking opportunities** with DEF CON attendees
- **No badge cost**—just show up and learn
- **Informal atmosphere** encourages participation and discussion

## BSides Philosophy

BSides events are community-organized, volunteer-run conferences that operate on the principle of "for the community, by the community." They provide a platform for speakers who might not make it into larger conferences and create space for diverse voices in security.

## Content Focus

- **Emerging technologies** and security implications
- **Community building** and diversity in security
- **Practical skills** for security practitioners
- **Career development** and industry trends
- **Research presentations** from community members

## Pro Tips

- **Arrive early**—seating is first-come, first-served
- **Great way to ease into DEF CON week**—arrive early, attend BSides, then dive into DEF CON
- **Many DEF CON attendees make BSides a tradition**—great networking opportunity
- **Free swag** from sponsors and community groups
- **Badge Price**: FREE (just register to help with headcount)

BSides LV is an excellent way to connect with the security community without the cost and intensity of the larger conferences. It's perfect for students, newcomers, or anyone looking for a more relaxed conference experience.
