## Conference Overview

DEF CON 34 returns to Las Vegas for four days of hacking, learning, and chaos. As one of the world's longest-running hacker conventions, DEF CON brings together security professionals, researchers, enthusiasts, and curious minds from around the globe.

## What to Expect

- **Cutting-edge security research presentations** across multiple tracks
- **Hands-on workshops and villages** (Lockpicking, Hardware Hacking, IoT, Wireless, Social Engineering)
- **Contests** including Capture the Flag, King of the Hill, and Social Engineering
- **Networking** with industry leaders and underground researchers
- **Vendor area** with latest security tools and exclusive swag
- **Late-night parties** and impromptu hacking sessions
