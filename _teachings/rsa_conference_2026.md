## Conference Overview

RSA Conference is the largest cybersecurity event in the world, attracting over 40,000 attendees including CISOs, security practitioners, vendors, and media. With its massive expo floor and comprehensive agenda, RSA represents the enterprise face of cybersecurity.

## What to Expect

- **Expo Floor**: 600+ vendors showcasing security products and services
- **Keynotes**: Industry leaders and government officials sharing insights
- **Sessions**: Technical and strategic tracks across all security domains
- **RSAC Early Stage Expo**: Startup showcase and innovation
- **Networking Events**: Receptions, parties, and meetups throughout SF

## Expo Highlights

- **Innovation Sandbox**: Top 10 startups compete for funding and recognition
- **Early Stage Expo**: Emerging companies showcase new technologies
- **Sand Hill East**: Enterprise security solutions and services
- **Sand Hill West**: Cutting-edge startups and disruptive technologies
- **Central Hall**: Major vendors and industry leaders

## Session Tracks

- Application Security & DevSecOps
- Cloud Security & Virtualization
- Data Security & Privacy
- Fraud & Cybercrime
- Governance, Risk & Compliance
- Identity & Access Management
- IoT & Operational Technology
- Security Strategy & Architecture
- Threat Detection & Response
- Zero Trust

## Pro Tips

- **Expo passes are often free** if you register early (use promo codes)
- **Hotel prices in SF skyrocket** during RSA—book 6+ months ahead
- **Wear comfortable shoes**—the Moscone Center is massive
- **Follow #RSAC on social media** for real-time updates and networking
- **Badge Price**: Full Conference ~$2,995, Expo Pass ~$0 (free)

While less technical than Black Hat or DEF CON, RSA is essential for understanding market trends, vendor landscapes, and enterprise security priorities.
