## Conference Overview

THOTCON (The Hacker On The Other THOTCON) is Chicago's flagship hacker conference, known for its high-quality talks, unique badge design, and vibrant community. Now in its 13th year, THOTCON has established itself as one of the Midwest's must-attend security events.

## What Makes THOTCON Special

- **Hardware Badges**: Each year features a custom-designed electronic badge
- **Single Track**: No scheduling conflicts—everyone sees every talk
- **Vendor Area**: Security tools, books, and unique hacker merchandise
- **After-Parties**: Legendary social events at Chicago venues
- **CTF**: Capture the Flag competition running throughout the conference

## Chicago Perks

- **World-class food scene** (deep dish pizza, anyone?)
- **More affordable** than Vegas or SF conferences
- **Central location** makes it accessible to much of the US
- **Spring timing** avoids winter travel issues
- **Public transportation** (CTA) makes getting around easy

## Conference Features

- **Two full days** of technical presentations
- **Hands-on villages** for practical learning
- **Vendor hall** with security tools and swag
- **Networking opportunities** with Midwest security community
- **Evening social events** at local venues

## Pro Tips

- **Badges are limited** and typically sell out within hours of release (usually in January)
- **Follow [@THOTCON](https://twitter.com/THOTCON)** for announcements and updates
- **Book hotels early**—blocks fill up quickly
- **Bring cash** for vendors and side events
- **Badge Price**: ~$200 (varies by purchase timing)

THOTCON has a reputation for being especially welcoming to first-time conference attendees while maintaining technical depth that satisfies veteran hackers.
