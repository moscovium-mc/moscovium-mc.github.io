RSA Conference 2026

The world's leading cybersecurity conference and exposition, bringing together industry leaders, security professionals, and technology innovators for four days of education, networking, and industry insights.

Instructor: RSA Conference Team

Term: Spring

Location: Moscone Center, San Francisco, California

Time: Monday, March 23 – Thursday, March 26, 2026

Event Overview

RSA Conference 2026 continues the tradition of the world’s most influential cybersecurity event, established in 1991. This four-day conference provides:

  • Keynote Presentations: Industry visionaries and thought leaders sharing insights on emerging threats and security trends
  • Breakout Sessions: Hundreds of sessions across multiple tracks including Security Strategy, Threat Detection & Response, Cloud Security, and Privacy
  • Expo Hall: 500+ exhibitors showcasing cutting-edge security products, services, and solutions
  • Innovation Sandbox: Competition featuring 10 emerging cybersecurity startups pitching to industry judges
  • Networking Opportunities: Exclusive events connecting security professionals, vendors, and industry leaders
  • Hands-On Labs: Interactive workshops providing practical experience with security tools and techniques

Registration Options

All-Access Pass

  • Full access to all keynotes, sessions, and Expo Hall
  • Entry to all networking events and receptions
  • OnDemand access to recorded sessions (typically 30 days post-event)
  • Early registration pricing available

Expo Pass (Free)

  • Access to Expo Hall and vendor demonstrations
  • Entry to select keynote presentations
  • Limited networking event access
  • Registration required (complimentary)

VIP Pass

  • All-Access benefits plus premium amenities
  • Dedicated registration and lounge access
  • Priority seating at keynotes and sessions
  • Exclusive VIP networking events

Note: Early bird registration typically opens in October 2025 with discounted pricing.

Innovation Sandbox Contest

The annual Innovation Sandbox contest showcases 10 of the most promising cybersecurity startups. Each company presents a 3-minute pitch to a panel of industry judges. The winner is recognized as “The Most Innovative Startup” at RSA Conference.

  • Application Period: Opens August 2025
  • Finalists Announced: January 2026
  • Contest Date: Tuesday, March 24, 2026
  • Past Winners: Include companies like Imperva, Sourcefire, and Cylance

RSAC OnDemand

All-Access Pass holders receive complimentary access to RSAC OnDemand, featuring:

  • Recorded sessions from all conference tracks
  • Keynote presentations and panel discussions
  • Expo Hall vendor presentations
  • Typically available within 48 hours of session completion
  • Access period: 30 days post-conference
