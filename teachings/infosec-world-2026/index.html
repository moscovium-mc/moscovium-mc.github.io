Event Overview

InfoSec World 2026 marks the 32nd annual gathering of security professionals to navigate past waves of change in strategy and tactics, tools and best practices. Now in 2026, it’s time for radical re-invention — of our teams, technologies, and ourselves.

Theme: “The Cyber Continuum: From Acceleration to Adaptation”

The conference addresses critical challenges facing the cybersecurity industry:

  • The AI Revolution: Empowering and emboldening our adversaries while creating new defensive opportunities
  • Proliferating Bad Actors: Threat actors becoming more professional and sophisticated
  • Regulatory Pressures: Multiplying legal and compliance requirements
  • C-Suite Involvement: Increasing executive concern and oversight of security programs
  • New Skills Required: Security professionals need to evolve to survive and thrive

Event Statistics

  • 200+ Speakers: Industry leaders, thought leaders, and innovators
  • 150+ Sessions: Breakout tracks, panels, and specialized presentations
  • 9+ Workshops: Pre- and post-conference intensive training
  • 2,500+ Attendees: Security professionals from all regions and industries
  • 6 Days Total: Including pre- and post-event workshops

Core Imperatives

InfoSec World 2026 is built around eight core imperatives designed to equip security professionals with critical skills:

1. Advance Your Cyber Career

Grow skills, credentials, and confidence in a rapidly evolving field

2. Anticipate the Threat Landscape

Decode emerging adversaries, tactics, and trends

3. Elevate Cyber Leadership

Inspire, influence, and execute with authority and vision

4. Lead Cyber as a Business Strategy

Align cybersecurity with business, risk, and board-level priorities

5. Modernize Governance, Risk & Compliance

Build agile, audit-ready GRC programs for the hybrid enterprise

6. Operationalize AI Securely

Harness artificial intelligence to advance defense—without compromising control

7. Secure the Core

Fortify infrastructure across cloud, hybrid, and on-prem environments

8. Secure the Software Supply Chain

Embed security into development pipelines and codebases
