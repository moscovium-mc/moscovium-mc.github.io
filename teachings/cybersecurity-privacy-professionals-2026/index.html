Cybersecurity and Privacy Professionals Conference 2026

The premier forum for connecting with higher education information security and privacy professionals. This event brings together peers and solution providers to discuss trends, challenges, and current issues in cybersecurity and privacy within the higher education sector.

Instructor: EDUCAUSE

Term: Spring

Location: Anaheim, California

Time: Tuesday, April 28 – Thursday, April 30, 2026

Event Overview

The Cybersecurity and Privacy Professionals Conference 2026 in Anaheim is the premier forum for connecting with higher education information security and privacy professionals. This is the one higher ed event that gives participants a chance to network and discuss information security and privacy trends and current issues with peers and solution providers.

Extended Programming

Due to overwhelming attendee interest, the conference programming has been extended to bring you even more valuable insights, expert discussions, and exclusive content. The conference now includes preconference workshops on the morning of April 28, followed by the opening general session and reception.

Conference Tracks & Topics

Cybersecurity Strategy & Governance

  • Security leadership and CISO perspectives
  • Risk management and compliance frameworks
  • Security architecture and design
  • Incident response planning and execution

Privacy & Data Protection

  • FERPA, HIPAA, and regulatory compliance
  • Data governance and classification
  • Privacy by design principles
  • Student data protection strategies

Threat Intelligence & Defense

  • Advanced persistent threats (APTs) in higher education
  • Ransomware defense and recovery
  • Security operations center (SOC) best practices
  • Threat hunting and detection techniques

Emerging Technologies

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning security
  • Cloud security in academic environments
  • Zero Trust architecture implementation
  • IoT and connected campus security

Higher Education Specific

  • Securing research data and intellectual property
  • Third-party vendor risk management
  • Student privacy and digital citizenship
  • Budget constraints and resource optimization

Preconference Workshops

Preconference workshops on the morning of April 28 offer in-depth, hands-on training on specialized topics. These workshops require separate registration and have limited seating.

Workshop Topics (Sample)

  • Incident Response Tabletop Exercise: Practice responding to realistic security incidents
  • Privacy Impact Assessment Workshop: Learn to conduct PIAs for campus initiatives
  • Cloud Security Architecture: Design secure cloud environments for higher ed
  • Threat Intelligence for Higher Education: Building effective threat intel programs
  • Security Awareness Program Development: Creating engaging security training

Who Should Attend

This conference is designed for higher education professionals responsible for cybersecurity and privacy, including:

  • Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs)
  • Information Security Directors and Managers
  • Privacy Officers and Compliance Managers
  • IT Directors and System Administrators
  • Network Security Engineers
  • Security Analysts and Specialists
  • Risk Management Professionals
  • Legal Counsel and Compliance Officers
  • Vendor Partners and Solution Providers
