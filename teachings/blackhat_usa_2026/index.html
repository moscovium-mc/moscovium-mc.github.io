Black Hat USA 2026

Premier information security conference and training. Black Hat brings together elite security researchers, corporate executives, government agencies, and security vendors for cutting-edge briefings and in-depth technical training.

Instructor: Black Hat

Term: Summer

Location: Mandalay Bay Convention Center, Las Vegas, NV

Time: August 2-7, 2026

Conference Overview

Black Hat USA is the world’s leading information security conference, bringing together elite security researchers, corporate executives, government agencies, and security vendors. Known for its rigorous research standards and high-profile briefings, Black Hat sets the tone for the security industry each year.

Conference Structure

  • Arsenal (Aug 2-3): Free tool demonstrations and presentations
  • Trainings (Aug 2-5): In-depth technical courses ($4,000-$6,000)
  • Business Hall (Aug 6-7): Vendor expo and networking
  • Briefings (Aug 6-7): Cutting-edge security research presentations

Key Features

  • Zero-day vulnerability disclosures and advanced exploit techniques
  • Enterprise security solutions showcase with 400+ vendors
  • Career fair and recruitment opportunities with top companies
  • Networking events with industry leaders and government officials
  • Black Hat Black Badge for elite researchers (invitation only)

Briefings Topics

  • Application Security
  • Cloud Security
  • Hardware Hacking
  • Industrial Control Systems
  • Malware Analysis
  • Mobile Security
  • Network Security
  • Social Engineering
  • Threat Intelligence
  • Zero Trust Architecture

Pro Tips

  • Early bird registration typically opens in January with significant discounts
  • Briefings passes are separate from trainings—plan accordingly
  • Business Hall passes are often free if you register early
  • Hotel prices in Vegas skyrocket during Black Hat—book 6+ months ahead
  • Badge Price: Briefings ~$3,500, Trainings ~$4,000-$6,000

Black Hat is the perfect precursor to DEF CON—more formal and enterprise-focused, but equally packed with groundbreaking research.
